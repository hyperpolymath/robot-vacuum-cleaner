## Becoming a Maintainer

### Path to Perimeter 2 (Trusted Collaborator)

Requirements:
1. **Sustained Contribution**: 3-6 months of regular contributions
2. **Quality**: Consistent high-quality code/documentation
3. **Community**: Active in reviews, discussions, helping others
4. **Expertise**: Deep knowledge in specific area
5. **Trust**: Demonstrated commitment and good judgment

Process:
1. Existing maintainers nominate candidate
2. Private discussion among current maintainers
3. Consensus decision (no objections)
4. Invitation extended to candidate
5. Onboarding and access granted

### Path to Perimeter 1 (Core Maintainer)

Requirements:
1. **Long-term Commitment**: 1+ year as Perimeter 2 maintainer
2. **Project Vision**: Aligned with project goals
3. **Leadership**: Mentors others, makes sound decisions
4. **Availability**: Consistent availability for critical decisions
5. **Trust**: Proven track record of excellent judgment

Process:
1. Unanimous agreement from existing Perimeter 1
2. Extended discussion period
3. Trial period (3 months)
4. Final decision and announcement

## Decision Making

### Consensus Model

- **Perimeter 3 → 2**: Consensus among Perimeter 1+2 (no blocking objections)
- **Technical Decisions**: Consensus among relevant area maintainers
- **Breaking Changes**: Requires Perimeter 1 approval
- **Emergency Fixes**: Can be fast-tracked by any maintainer

### Conflict Resolution

1. **Discussion**: Try to reach consensus
2. **Mediation**: Perimeter 1 maintainer mediates
3. **Vote**: If consensus fails, Perimeter 1 votes
4. **Final**: Lead maintainer has final say

### Voting Procedures

When votes are necessary:
- All Perimeter 1 maintainers can vote
- Simple majority (>50%)
- Quorum: 2/3 of Perimeter 1
- Tied vote: Lead maintainer decides

## Stepping Down

Maintainers can step down at any time:

1. **Notify**: Email other maintainers privately
2. **Transition**: Help transfer responsibilities (2-4 weeks)
3. **Access**: Repository access revoked after transition
4. **Recognition**: Listed as Emeritus Maintainer
5. **Return**: Can rejoin later with reduced ramp-up

## Inactivity Policy

### Automatic Downgrade

- **Perimeter 2**: No activity for 6 months → move to Perimeter 3
- **Perimeter 1**: No activity for 6 months → discuss among remaining P1

### Re-activation

Former maintainers can return:

- Must demonstrate renewed commitment
- Discussion among current maintainers
- May skip some requirements based on history
